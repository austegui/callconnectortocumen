How the call flow works

The implementation is intentionally clean: browser audio on the front end, Twilio token issuance and voice routing on the back end, and a single hosted asset for the client to drop in.

1 Client adds the script

The website includes your hosted `embed.js`, along with route and branding options.

2 Widget loads instantly

Your script injects the iframe experience and requests microphone permission only when the visitor actually starts a call.

3 Server mints the token

Your backend creates the Twilio Voice access token and enforces the destination rules you define.

4 Twilio connects the call

The browser call is bridged through your TwiML App to the correct agent, queue, SIP target, or phone number.
